About Us

As Group 7, our team comprises four passionate members: Oliver Rathbone, Jake Pinos, Tyler Dunphy, and Amaury Couturier. Together, we embarked on a mission to develop a nutrition-focused application that empowers users to effortlessly track their daily macronutrient intake. Our primary goal was to create a user-friendly and accessible tool that caters to people from all walks of life.

Drawing inspiration from the everyday needs of the average person, we set out to design a seamless and intuitive experience. Our vision was not to reinvent the wheel but to provide a practical solution that resonates with individuals seeking a straightforward way to manage their nutrition effectively.

By leveraging our diverse skill sets and combining our passion for health and technology, we aimed to craft an application that simplifies the process of monitoring macronutrients. Our app offers an inclusive platform for users to stay mindful of their dietary choices and achieve their nutritional goals with ease.
